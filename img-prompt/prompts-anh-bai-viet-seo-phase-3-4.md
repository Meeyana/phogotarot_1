Ghi chu chung:
- Dung cho anh hero/thumbnail bai viet blog, ti le 16:9, goi y xuat 1600x900 px hoac 1920x1080 px.
- Phong cach chung cua Phở Gõ Tarot: mystical editorial, tarot table setup, dark navy background, warm gold accents, soft candlelight, subtle stardust, premium but approachable.
- Khong de chu trong anh. Neu can title, them bang code/CSS tren website sau.
